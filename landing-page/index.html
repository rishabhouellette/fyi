Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FYIXT.

How is FYIXT different from Buffer or Hootsuite?
Those tools just schedule posts. FYIXT is an all-in-one command center — it creates content with AI (captions, images, videos), schedules it, tracks analytics, generates faceless videos, manages short links, and supports BYOK (bring your own API keys). It replaces 8+ separate tools in one dashboard.
What exactly are workflows?
Workflows let you set up automated sequences that cross-post, transform, and schedule your content across platforms. AI handles resizing, platform-specific captions, and optimal timing — so you create once and FYIXT does the rest.
Which social media platforms are supported?
Instagram, Threads, YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Bluesky, X (Twitter), Snapchat, and more. We add new platforms regularly. Connect once, post everywhere.
Does scheduling posts hurt my views?
No. All major platforms are interest-based now, not follower-based. What matters is quality content, not whether you clicked "post" manually. Many creators with millions of followers schedule 100% of their posts. Our AI even picks the optimal time for max reach.
What is "Bring Your Own Keys" (BYOK)?
You can plug in your own API keys for OpenAI, Gemini, Claude, ElevenLabs, Stability AI, and 6 more services. Full control over costs and AI model choice. No keys? No problem — FYIXT includes built-in AI out of the box.
Can I manage multiple client accounts?
Absolutely. The Agency plan supports multi-client workspaces — manage separate projects, client accounts, or brands from one dashboard. Switch between workspaces in a click.
Who is FYIXT NOT for?
FYIXT is NOT for automated spam or low-effort AI dumps. It's built for creators, brands, and agencies who care about quality and want to grow efficiently. If you're looking for a "post 1000 AI articles overnight" bot, this isn't it. If you want to build a real brand 10× faster — welcome home.
How secure is my data?
Security is a top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption, token-based authentication, and strict data protection. Your API keys are encrypted at rest. We do NOT sell your data — ever.
